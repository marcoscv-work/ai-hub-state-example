AI Hub is mid-commercialization, moving open beta to paid public beta

This report cross-references four live sources: design in the AI Hub Figma project, delivery in Jira, engineering in the liferay-ai-hub/liferay-portal fork, and the ai-hub module in the Liferay codebase.
